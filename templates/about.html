Our Mission

Our goal is to provide early detection of heart stroke risk using machine learning technology, helping individuals take preventive actions that can save lives.

How It Works

The system analyzes critical factors such as age, blood pressure, BMI, glucose levels, and more to calculate your risk level.

Technology Behind It

Built with Python, Flask, and trained machine learning models (scikit-learn, TensorFlow), our solution delivers accurate predictions based on real-world data.

Privacy and Trust

Your data is never stored. The system performs analysis in real-time and discards input afterward.
