Our Mission

The Stroke Prediction System is an AI-driven healthcare project aimed at helping individuals assess their risk of stroke through a simple, accessible web interface. Early detection of risk factors can significantly reduce the impact of strokes and save lives.

How It Works

Our system uses a machine learning model trained on real-world health data to analyze key indicators such as:

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Hypertension and Heart Disease History
  • Average Glucose Level
  • Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Smoking Status
  • Residence Type

Based on the data you provide, the system calculates the likelihood of a stroke and provides a result instantly. This can help you take proactive steps by consulting a healthcare provider.

Why It Matters

Strokes are a leading cause of death and disability worldwide. However, they are often preventable through early lifestyle changes and medical intervention. By offering a quick and easy way to assess your risk, our system empowers users to take charge of their health.

Technology Behind It

The Stroke Prediction System is powered by Python, Flask, and machine learning models built using scikit-learn or TensorFlow. The model has been trained on thousands of anonymized patient records to ensure accurate predictions.
